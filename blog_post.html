My Favorite National Parks

The first United States of America's National Park was created by President Ulysses Grant. On March 1, 1872 President Grant signed the Yellowstone National Park Protection Act into Law. This created our first national park, Yellowstone!

Yellowstone's beauty is truly breath taking. The melting of colors of Yellowstone's hot springs, like the Morning Glory Spring, draws vistors from all over the world

Another famous draw to Yellowstone national park is the geyser known as Old Faithful. Which erupts on a regular base. This geyser gets it heat and energy from something undernearth Yellowstone most people do not know, the Yellowstone Supervolcano.

Volcanos are one of natures more powerful resetters. History books are filled with stories similiar to the famous Pompeii fatal end. The Yellowstone Supervolcano was estimated to have last erupted 640 thosand years ago. It's power is believe to be a destory of life on North America's future.
